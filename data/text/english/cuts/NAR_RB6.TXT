1:After Hog's death, feuds and power struggles started in Rainbow. No one wanted to obey anyone and chaos erupted in the settlement.
2:During one of this armed confrontation, Naomi Young escaped from the lab, and the production of the Drop and other drugs in the Rainbow stopped completely.
3:Merchants didn't visit the settlement anymore - there was nothing of value for them there. The supply of water and food in the place became scarce.
4:The inhabitants of Rainbow were forced to find another place to live. Many of them, due to weakness and hunger, never made it to the other settlements.
