1:After Hog's death, power struggles began in Rainbow. No one wanted to obey anyone, and chaos erupted in the settlement.
2:During one of these armed confrontations, Naomi Young escaped from the lab, and the production of the Drop and other drugs in Rainbow came to a complete halt.
3:Merchants no longer visited the settlement--there was nothing of value to them there. Water and food supplies in the place became scarce.
4:The inhabitants of Rainbow were forced to find another place to live. Because of weakness and hunger, many of them were never able to reach other settlements.
