1:Shortly after the fall of Sunset Jackals, the inhabitants of Rainbow decided to take over the military bases of Radius. But without skills and heavy weaponry they were unable to succeed.
2:Due to the lack of weapons in Radius, forceful methods of resolving issues became a thing of the past, and peaceful solutions got prevalent.
3:Trade and mutual cooperation started to develop, leading Radius to economical development. The Rainbow soon became famous for its excellent casinos and brothels.
